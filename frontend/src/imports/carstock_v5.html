Our Mission

Making Car Care Simple for Every Indian

Founded in 2020, CarStock began with a simple insight — getting your car serviced in India was unnecessarily stressful, expensive, and opaque. We set out to change that.

Today, CarStock connects thousands of vehicle owners with background-verified, IAI-certified mechanics who come to your doorstep. We also operate India's fastest-growing mechanic-inspected used car marketplace.

We believe car care should be as simple, transparent, and trustworthy as ordering food online.
